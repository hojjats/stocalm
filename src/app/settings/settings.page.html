About us

Stocalm

 Putting calm to your everyday big city living Stocalm started up in 2019, and we've since the beginning been on a truly speechless ride. What was from the beginning our idea to solve the issues we saw with living in a big city such as ours truly loved Stockholm, has now exploded and evolved to the very application you are using as you read this.

We wanted to create something useful, meaningful and above all something helpful for our fellow co-citizens. We owe a huge thanks to the contributors and supporters of this project, we wouldn't be us nor here without you.

Let's continue the ride together and make Stockholm better for all.
